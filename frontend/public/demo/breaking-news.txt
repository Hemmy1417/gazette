MAYOR RAMIREZ RESIGNS: cites desire to protect the city from distraction

The mayor of Riverbrook, Elena Ramirez, announced her resignation today,
effective at the end of the month, following a six-month investigation into
the awarding of city construction contracts to firms tied to her family.

"After deep reflection I have decided the city's business must come before
any one person," Ramirez said in a brief written statement. She took no
questions and made no reference to contesting the allegations.

Ramirez's attorneys are reported to be in discussions with the state attorney
general's office. The office declined to comment on the pending matter but
said the investigation remains active. A special election will be scheduled.
