BREAKING: Mayor Ramirez announces immediate resignation

The mayor of Riverbrook, Elena Ramirez, announced her resignation this morning,
effective at noon Friday. The announcement follows a six-month investigation
into the awarding of city construction contracts to firms tied to her family.

"I am stepping down to ensure the integrity of this office," Ramirez said in
a written statement. "The people of Riverbrook deserve confidence in their
government." She did not take questions.

Deputy Mayor Farid Ostrowski will serve as acting mayor until a special
election is held on March 14. The investigation, led by the state attorney
general's office, is expected to continue into next quarter.
